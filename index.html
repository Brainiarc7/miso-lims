Thank you for your interest in MISO! You can get to the source code by cloning the repo via the "View on GitHub" link above, or by downloading a zip or tarball of the project using the relevant button above. If you'd like a simpler setup route to getting MISO up and running, please see our page on the TGAC website.

MISO was first released on the 22nd June 2012 in the centenary year of the birth of Alan Turing, on the eve of his birthday, in celebration of his contribution to computing.
